The Challenge: The Old Program

Our previous bonus structure was simple but inefficient. It rewarded tenure over performance and created significant hidden costs and a lack of clear accountability.

Major Flaw: Cost Leakage

The biggest issue was paying all certified trainers on a shift for a single new hire. This multiplied costs without guaranteeing individual effort.

The Financial Impact

By shifting from tenure to performance and eliminating redundant payouts, the new program represents a smarter, more efficient use of resources.

Direct Savings: Paying for Results

We now avoid uncontrolled costs by paying a single, accountable trainer only when a new hire meets clear performance, safety, and conduct standards.

Indirect Savings: Long-Term ROI

The true value emerges over time. Better training directly reduces costs associated with turnover, safety incidents, and lost productivity.
